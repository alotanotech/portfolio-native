About Me

Christopher Aurellyan Alotano Luna

Hello! I'm Christopher Aurellyan Alotano Luna, an Informatics student with a strong passion for graphic design, branding, and front-end development. I enjoy transforming ideas into visual experiences that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing.

My creative journey started from curiosity. Designing posters, social media content, branding materials, and websites gradually became more than just a hobby. Today, I strive to combine creativity with technology, allowing me to create digital products that communicate effectively while maintaining strong visual appeal.

Beyond software proficiency, I value problem solving, consistency, and continuous learning. Every project gives me an opportunity to improve my design thinking, refine my workflow, and understand how thoughtful design can positively impact a brand or business.

My long-term goal is to build a career where design, development, and creativity intersect, whether through freelance work, collaborative projects, or creating digital experiences that people genuinely enjoy using.
